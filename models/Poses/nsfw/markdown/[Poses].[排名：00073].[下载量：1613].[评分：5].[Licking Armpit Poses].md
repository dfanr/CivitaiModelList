And if you don't, well your loss :D<br /></p><p>2.0: Added 2 more closeup poses. New category, full body, with 5 poses added. Reorganized and renamed files for better overview, also included a preview for every pose for easy reference. All poses feature normal, openpose and softedge maps, standardized their resolutions to 1536 x ca. 2200-2300. btw, gfpgan is recommended for face restoration, codeformers seems to have problems with sideway faces. oh, and for softedge maps setting controlnet to "balanced" seems to create the worst images, at least for me. sometimes playing around with controlnet weight settings can help alot.</p><p><strong>1.2:</strong> Softedge variants added. They often work great, but sometimes they seem to completely break controlnet. Be prepared for everything :D As a special bonus for surpassing 100 likes with this upload i also created a hairy version of all softedge maps. They don't work out of the box, you still need to prompt for armpit hair, probably with some emphasis. Don't expect them to work on first try, though. hf ;)</p><p><strong>1.1:</strong> Mirrored variants for initial poses added.
