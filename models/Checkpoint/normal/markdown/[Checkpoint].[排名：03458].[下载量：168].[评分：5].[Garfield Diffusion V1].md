<p>My first big model. Put <code>sunday</code> in positive prompt.</p></li></ul><p>Use <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/hakurei/waifu-diffusion-v1-4/blob/main/vae/kl-f8-anime2.ckpt">kl-f8-anime2 VAE</a></p>
