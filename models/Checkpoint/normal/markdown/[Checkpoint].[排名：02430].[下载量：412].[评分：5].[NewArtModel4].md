### 三、详情
<h2><strong>Why NewArtModel?</strong></h2><p>The model itself is mainly based on <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/hakurei/waifu-diffusion"><strong><u>Waifu-Diffusion 1.3</u></strong></a> and was finetuned using a dataset of my own artwork and collected artwork of others.</p><p>I hope you enjoy the model. If you have any questions, you can ask me anything via Discord: "Nerfgun3#7508"</p><p></p><h2><strong>Use Cases</strong></h2><p>The model itself is very good in itself and produces high quality images from landscapes to portraits to anime. However, it shines the most when merged with other models.</p><p>Here are a few examples:</p><ol><li><p>Generally all models from <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/andite"><strong><u>andite</u></strong></a></p></li><li><p>Kenshi Model from <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/SweetLuna/Kenshi"><strong><u>Luna</u></strong></a></p></li></ol><p></p><h2><strong>Token</strong></h2><p>This model was trained on a token <strong>m_style</strong> like the Dreambooth models, but as can be seen in the sample prompts, it is not needed, and since merging is one of its strengths, it is not essential.</p>
