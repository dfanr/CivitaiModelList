### 三、详情
<p><strong>This is </strong><a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/closertodeath/mouseymix"><strong>mousymix</strong></a><strong>, refined. This is mouseyMIX-<em>Pro</em>.</strong></p><p>Mouseymix is an little known merge model from ClosertoDeath, who is a model-trainer who made some more well known merges, such as pastelmix, HoloKukiV2, or SomethingV2_2, possible. It is a powerful but finicky checkpoint, extremely overfit due to the nature of its construction, but unparalleled in affecting the style that it does. Its unique style that has inspired with more refined tastes to attempt leverage its strength while discarding its weaknesses through merging. <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://civitai.com/models/14734/">Gyozamix</a>. <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://civitai.com/models/10095/">FantaStel</a>. <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://civitai.com/models/36591">Osenayan</a>,<a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://civitai.com/models/14206"> MIX-Pro</a>. But inevitably they all diverge from mouseymix's unique style and strengths and begin to lose what makes mouseymix what it is. <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://civitai.com/models/7241/mix-pro-v4">MIX-Pro-V4.5+Colorbox</a> is the purest of the mouseymix descendants but inevitably even then, it cannot retain mousemix's pure style thanks to the amount of merges and changes needed to reduce the amount of overfitting in mousy.</p><p>But what if we just merged mouseymix back into MIX-Pro?</p><p>Utilizing new style-strip composition-retention block weights from cyberwaifuroon, mouseyMIX-Pro applies mousey-correction onto MIX-Pro, thereby allowing us to utilize MIX-Pro's fantastic versatility and responsiveness to prompts while retaining mouseymix's style. The result is a highly opinionated and powerful yet versatile style model that hewns more closely to mouseymix than other checkpoint besides mouseymix, itself.</p><p><strong>MIX-Pro returns to Mousey. <em>mouseyMIX</em>-Pro.</strong></p><p><a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/LMFResearchSociety/CheckpointStyleTheft/settings">Style-transfer proof-of-concept HuggingFace repository with block-merge weights</a></p>
