### 三、详情
<p>This model was based on <a href="https://huggingface.co/naclbit/trinart_stable_diffusion_v2" rel="ugc" target="_blank">Trinart</a> model.</p><p>The dataset was a collaborative effort of the Stable Diffusion #anime channel, made of pictures from the users themselves using their different techniques.</p><p>100 total pictures in the dataset, 300 repeats total each, over 6 Epoch on LR1e-6.</p><p>This was trained using EveryDream with a full caption of all training pictures.</p><p>The style will be called by the use of the token AnimeChan Style.</p>
