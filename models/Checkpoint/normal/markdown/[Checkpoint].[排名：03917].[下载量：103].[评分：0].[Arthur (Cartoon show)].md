### 三、详情
<p>A silly fine tuning of SDv1.5 upon <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://twitter.com/arthur_screens">@arthur_screens</a>  which is a "No context Arthur screenshots with Hebrew subtitles" account. Notice that this model is based on a 1996 cartoon show screenshots so expect your generated images to look like a 1996 cartoon show screenshots with gibberish subtitles. </p><p>Model is also <a rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/Norod78/sd15-arthur-blip-captions">available on Hugging Face</a> in Diffusers format</p>
