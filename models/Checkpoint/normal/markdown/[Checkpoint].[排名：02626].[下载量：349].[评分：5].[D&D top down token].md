### 三、详情
<h1>D&amp;D top down token</h1><h2>Resolution</h2><p>Model was trained on 512x512 images, so it works best on this resolution.</p><h2>Prompting</h2><p>Model was trained on D&amp;D monsters, heroes and master characters (NPCs). So it works best for medieval fantasy setting. It's recommended to use trigger words, that define monster type.</p><p>But it's not overtrained only on this setting, as for me works pretty good with almost everything I tried. You can not use trigger words at all, that would be fine too, sometimes that's even better.</p><p>Also you can try mix them to get interesting results.
