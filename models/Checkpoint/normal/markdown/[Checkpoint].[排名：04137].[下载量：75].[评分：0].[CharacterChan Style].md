### 三、详情
<p>This model was based on <a href="https://huggingface.co/runwayml/stable-diffusion-v1-5" rel="ugc" target="_blank">RunwayML SD 1.5</a> model with updated VAE.</p><p>The dataset was a collaborative effort of the Stable Diffusion discord #CharacterChan channel, made of pictures from the users themselves using their different techniques.</p><p>50 total pictures in the dataset, 160 repeats total each, over 4 Epoch on LR1e-6.</p><p>This was trained using EveryDream with a full caption of all training pictures.</p><p>The style will be called by the use of the token CharacterChan Style.</p>
