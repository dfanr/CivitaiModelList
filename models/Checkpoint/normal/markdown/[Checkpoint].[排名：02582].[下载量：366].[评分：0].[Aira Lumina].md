### 三、详情
<p>"Aira Lumina" is a <em>huge</em> additive model merge, as can be seen by its ~4gb fp16 size. It carefully combines well over 200 models, including both realistic and anime checkpoints. </p><p>This has been a labor of love, often doing and undoing merges based on the results. Probably the most impressive thing is the integrity of the results -- you have less control over whether you get anime/photorealistic photos, but it passes <a rel="ugc" href="https://www.reddit.com/user/alexds9">alexds9</a>'s <a rel="ugc" href="https://www.reddit.com/r/StableDiffusion/comments/12u6c76/can_we_identify_most_stable_diffusion_model/">overfit/corruption test</a> with flying colors.</p><p>Because the merge is mostly additive, it performs well at both clipskip 1 and 2, and flipping back and forth can yield interesting results.</p><p>Enjoy!</p>
