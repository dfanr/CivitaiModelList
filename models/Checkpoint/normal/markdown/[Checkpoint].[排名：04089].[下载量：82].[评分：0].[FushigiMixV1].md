### 三、详情
<p>A Mix of <a rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/ThePioneer/CoolerWaifuDiffusion">CoolerWaifuDiffusion7030</a> and <a rel="ugc" href="https://huggingface.co/stabilityai/stable-diffusion-2-1-base">SD 2.1 base (512)</a>.</p><p>It can generate more ani-real images than the original CoolerWaifu, or WaifuDiffusion and Cool Japan Diffusion, which are the origins of CoolerWaifu.</p><p><br />My final goal on this series is to get something close to <a rel="ugc" href="https://civitai.com/models/4936/moesharpv1">MoeSharpV1</a> only with NAI leak free SD 2.x based models, and from that point of view, it's still at the developing stage; further improvements may come in the future.<br /></p><p>It does NOT contain NAI leak based models (nor is trained on NAI leak generated images), unlike most of the SD 1.x based anime/waifu models.</p>
