The token stays "SDArt" and I balance the learning on the low side, so that it doesn't just replicate creations.</p><p></p><p><strong>The pictures were tagged using the token "SDArt", and an arbitrary token I choose for each submission</strong>.</p><p></p><p>The dataset is available below and is composed of 42 pictures.</p><p></p><h2>This week's theme</h2><p>Walking endlessly through your “Somewhere”, you begin to notice a variety of patterns and shapes. You watched from afar as two children ran freely through the petals, giggling merrily without a care in the world. You watched as a stray cat slept peacefully under a tree. You watched as your surroundings became gentle. Nostalgic. Beautiful.</p><p>Suddenly, your hand was tugged lightly. You turned around to face Someone. This warmth–this feeling.</p><p>It was the sun.</p><p>Your journey has reached its conclusion, Traveler…</p><ul><li><p>”Someone” is the one who reaches out to you when you feel abandoned, overwhelmed, and lost. “Someone” pulls you out of “Something” and “Somewhere”. “Someone” brings you back to reality.</p></li><li><p>How would your mind represent your “Someone”, express the feelings they evoke through your art</p></li><li><p>Show us who brings you into “Now”, giving you purpose with their presence and finding “Everything”</p></li></ul><p>This week's example photos were made with love by @andrekerygma and I!
