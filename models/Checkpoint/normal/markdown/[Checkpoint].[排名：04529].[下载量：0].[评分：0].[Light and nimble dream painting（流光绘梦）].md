### 三、详情
<p>这个模型被命名为“流光绘梦”，因为它的风格让我想起了延时摄影中的水流，并且我希望每个人都能用它来积极地画出梦境。</p><p>该模型使用了大量绘画截图作为材料，导致人物在2.5d的基础上发生了显著的风格变化。为了中和画笔给皮肤带来的粗糙感，我调整了重量，以保留2.5d的皮肤纹理，使整体设计具有更精致的手绘风格。</p><p>在修复变形的手时，建议添加：完美的手，美丽的手，</p><p>同时，添加负面提示，如变形的手和扭曲的手。</p><p>建议启用高分辨率修复。</p><p></p><p>The model is named "Light and Flowing Dream Painting" because its style reminds me of the water flow in time lapse photography, and I hope everyone can use it to actively draw dream scenes.</p><p>The model uses a lot of screenshots of paintings as materials, resulting in a significant stylistic change in the characters based on 2.5d. In order to neutralize the roughness brought by the brush to the skin, I adjusted the weight to retain a 2.5d skin texture, giving the overall design a more delicate hand drawn style.</p><p>When repairing deformed hands, it is recommended to add: perfect hands, beautiful hands,</p><p>At the same time, add negative prompts such as deformed hands and twisted hands.</p><p>It is recommended to enable high-resolution repair.</p>
