### 三、详情
<p>This model functions as a bit of a hybrid compared to my others. Based on loras and prompting, it can handle 2D, near-2.5D, and everything in-between!<br /><br />My usual settings:</p><p>Sampler: DPM++ 2M Karras<br />Sample Steps: 20-35<br />Upscaler: ESRGAN_4x<br />Deniose: 0.5<br />CFG: 5-9<br />Vae: Grapefruit vae<br /><br />By no means is this law, feel free to experiment and I'd love to see your results!!</p><p>For inpainting, I generally limit to Only Masked to 800 x 800 or lower and 0.5 denoise (or lower if you want to maintain the initial face look more). It will start to get a little too detailed at higher res'. You shouldn't need to change your prompt at all when inpainting faces to get a good result.<br /><br />Same as my other models, it plays very well with artist style loras!</p>
