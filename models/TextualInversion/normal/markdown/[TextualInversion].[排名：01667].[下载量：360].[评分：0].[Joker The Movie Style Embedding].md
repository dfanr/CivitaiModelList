### 三、详情
<p>This textual inversion is not focused on Joker as a subject, but rather on visual style, colors, and lighting. There are two ways to use it:</p><ol><li><p>Generate images of men (it picked up actors from the movie) or interiors and city landscapes. Examples above.</p></li><li><p>Add it as a secondary style to your main style to add a variety of colors and lighting to the image. Better use, a few examples shared.</p></li></ol><p>To use it, download it to your embeddings folder in Web UI installation and start the prompt with <strong><em>jokermovie style</em></strong> or <strong>style of jokermovie</strong>.</p><p>Enjoy and share the results in comments!</p><p></p>
