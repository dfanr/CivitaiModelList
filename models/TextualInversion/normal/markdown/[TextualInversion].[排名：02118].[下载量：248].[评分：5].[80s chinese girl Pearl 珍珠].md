### 三、详情
<p>In this new textual inversion, I aimed to create a beautiful woman that adheres to the aesthetic standards of the 1980s in China. In the 1980s, girls with this kind of appearance would usually appear as magazine or calendar models. In our setting, Pearl珍珠 came from the southern region of China and went to Shenzhen with the tide of reform and opening up in the 1980s, and established a ready-to-wear clothing store there.</p><p></p><p>Pearl is not a real person.</p><p></p><p>If you like my work, feel free to buy me a cup of coffee through ko-fi.com/dalecell,it will inspire me to create better work.</p>
