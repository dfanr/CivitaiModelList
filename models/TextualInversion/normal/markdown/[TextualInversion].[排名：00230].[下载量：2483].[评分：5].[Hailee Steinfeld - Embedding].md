### 三、详情
<p>Hailee Steinfeld embedding. I've had this set of training data sitting around for weeks, but prior attempts never worked out very well. This one still isn't perfect, and works much better in some models than others (particularly in Epic Diffusion, SD 1.5 and RinaMix2). There is a good TI already up, but I wanted one capable of slightly more realism without facerestore.</p><p>38 photos, 6750 steps, 2x2 batch size/gradient for a total 749 epochs</p>
