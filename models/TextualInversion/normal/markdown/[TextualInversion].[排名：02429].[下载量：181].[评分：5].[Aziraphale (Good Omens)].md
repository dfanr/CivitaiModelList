### 三、详情
<p>This textual inversion is based on the character of Aziraphale from Good Omens, as played by Michael Sheen. Since TIs draw on what SD already knows, it struggles with his soft and heavy build, wrinkles and dimples, and some outfit details like his tartan bow — I have made a LoRA intended to be used alongside this TI for this reason, and it addresses those issues.</p><p>Tips: I've found this to work best with the Dreamshaper and Realbiter models.</p>
