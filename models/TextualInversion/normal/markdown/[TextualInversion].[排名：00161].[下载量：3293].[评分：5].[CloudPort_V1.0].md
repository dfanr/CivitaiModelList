### 三、详情
<p>This textual inversion creates beautiful swirling coral and cloudlike patterns and blends into the heads of human and some furry animal subjects. Low CFG around 4-6 seems to be working best and if you don't want the odd greenish coloring that sometimes shows up adding the word "aqua" to the negative prompts helps a lot.</p><p>Feedback, tips, ideas, and sharing of prompts and outputs would be greatly appreciated. I've been messing around with this textual inversion a little bit but I know the creativity of this community far exceeds my own. I'd love to see what you all come up with.</p><p>Thanks for checking it out, Enjoy!</p>
