### 三、详情
<p>This textual inversion (TI) adds pastel colours, floral patterns and geometric motifs. I trained it using photographs of spray-painted street art from my local area. It has a tendency to create symmetrical compositions and a strong preference for circles and curves over straight lines and right-angles.</p><p></p><p>The TI does well at paintings and stylised images, but I have also had good results with photorealistic outputs.</p>
