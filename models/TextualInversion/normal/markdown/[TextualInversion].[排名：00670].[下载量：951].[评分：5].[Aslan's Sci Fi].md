### 三、详情
<p>These embeddings are trained on classic Sci-fi action movies from the 70s to the 2000s. I ultimately wanted something that could combine the classic scifi aesthetic and the realism of live action cinematography.</p><p>--------</p><p><strong>To use these embeddings in AUTOMATIC1111, copy them to your embeddings folder in your stable-diffusion-webui directory. No need to restart, just invoke the embedding from your prompt. Make sure you are using a compatible model or you will get errors!</strong></p><p>--------</p><p>The Sci-fi embeddings were all trained on 465 768x768 images that were cropped and tagged using BLIP for training.<br />--------</p><p>Like most other 2.1 textual inversions this works best when paired up with other embeddings and good negative prompting.</p>
