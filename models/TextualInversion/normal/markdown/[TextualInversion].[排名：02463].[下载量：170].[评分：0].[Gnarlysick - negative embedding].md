### 三、详情
<p>Oh look, another negative embedding. This one isn't anything amazing, just something I made for personal use and figured I would share.</p><p></p><p>So what does it do? Make the colours more bold, sharpen the image a little, and make the composition/details more cohesive. It doesn't necessarily add more detail, as it may even remove some to try and make the composition cleaner / more aesthetically pleasing. It will also try to make creatures/animals more natural looking (sorta).</p><p></p><p>Oh well, give it a try. If you like it, cool, hope it helps. If not, meh- no skin off my back. loll<br />Sample grids are made with various random models, same seed showing with and without the embed as the only negative. No highres-fix, no facefix, no touchups, just so you can see the negative effects better.</p>
