### 三、详情
<p>This is a textual inversion embedding to generate doggystyle sex positions with a focus on the arm(s) being pulled back, from a side-on/profile view. Use <code>corneo_side_doggy</code> to activate.</p><p><strong>This is a lot more temperamental than my other sex position embeddings</strong> (hands are really difficult), but when it does work the results can be great. It was trained exclusively with arm-grab sex facing to the left side of the image, so the vast majority will generate that way but you can get some others in there depending on your tags. You can always flip the image if you want it facing the other way. An interesting quirk of the way this embedding works is that the position will depend on the width of the image - tall images will have the position be more upright, while wider ones will be more horizontal/doggystyle. It can also do spitroast mmf threesome with a little encouragement.</p><p>Example images produced with OrangeCocoa and featuring characters from my other embeds, you can find both on my profile <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://civitai.com/user/Master_Corneo">https://civitai.com/user/Master_Corneo</a></p><p>If you enjoy this embed, I genuinely love seeing your creations with it - you can attach them to a review if you're not aware (but not comments sadly)!</p><p></p>
