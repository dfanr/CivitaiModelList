<p>This is a Textual Inversion embedding to add nipple rings to nipples! It's a very tricky one to get right, <strong>it's not as good/consistent as my other released embeddings</strong>, but I've had a number of non-character-embed failures in a row now so I wanted to put one of them out there!</p><p>It is quite wonky and won't look correct on probably even the majority of generations using it so fair warning. It's specifically trained on nipple <strong>rings</strong> so won't work for general piercings, though you could try mixing it with normal <code>nipple_piercing</code> tags. It should work for most characters though, doesn't just have to be female breasts, and it might benefit from a higher step count than normal.
