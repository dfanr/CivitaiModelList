### 三、详情
<p>Sometimes 2 legs isn't enough. Sometimes 4 legs isn't enough. Spiderlegs has you covered...in legs. This LoRA jams in legs and is best run in batches for the initial generation. I find a LoRA weight of 1-1.3, combined with ((spdlgs, extra legs):1.4) and squatting/sitting works well for txt2img. With img2img you can tone the LoRA down to 0.5ish. It's been trained on the tag spdlgs since the word spider gets you spooderman in a lot of models.</p>
