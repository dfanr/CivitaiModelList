<p>LoRA trained to generate undressing a character by pulling or lifting clothes.</p><p><strong><u>Please rate and leave a like if you enjoyed it! And be sure to follow to check out the upcoming models.</u></strong></p><p>This one is a bit more complicated than usual because of the diverse dataset, multiple poses and tags. You may need to combine tags and change weights and sometimes even seeds to get a good result. It may work very differently across base models, I haven't tested.</p><p>The easiest to generate is <u>skirt/dress lift</u>: <strong><em>skirt lift</em></strong> or <strong><em>dress lift</em></strong> , <strong><em>lifted by another</em></strong>, <strong><em>hands</em></strong>. And you can add <strong><em>looking back</em></strong>, or <strong><em>from behind</em></strong> or <strong><em>ass</em></strong>, to generate a view from behind.</p><p>For the rest you can try a combination of the following tags:</p><ul><li><p>Pulling: <strong><em>clothes pull, panties pull, shirt pull, pants pull, bra pull</em></strong>, etc.</p></li><li><p>Lifting: <strong><em>clothes lift, shirt lift, sweater lift, bra lift</em></strong>, etc.</p></li><li><p>Useful to add (one or more): <strong><em>pulled by another, lifted by another, undressing another, hands, pov hands, multiple hands</em></strong>, etc.</p></li></ul><p>Depending on how many tags you use it may start to get a slight "overfit" look so you may need to drop the lora weight under 1.0. And always use hires fix!</p><p>I typically prefer more straightforward loras with one trigger word but it didn't seem feasible with so many small variations.
