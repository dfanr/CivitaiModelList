### 三、详情
<p>This LoRA is intended to simulate more realistic bulges when characters are wearing skirts and dresses. It generally performs better with skirts, but can still generate dressed based bulges, but may require more finetuning and more generations. It can do various poses but prefers standing or sitting poses as this was the majority of images used to train this LoRA. </p><p></p><p>The basic prompt is <code>skirt</code> and <code>cloth bulge</code>, but you can use <code>dress</code> as well. Various types of skirts and dresses such as pleated or pencil also work, though results may vary. Using <code>erection under clothing</code> does sometimes help, but you may need to add <code>penis</code> to the negative if you start seeing unwelcome penises appearing. This model can generate male characters as well, though expect them to appear more feminine due to nature of dataset and prompt.</p>
