<p>This LoRA generates a image of a walk with your pet on a nice day.</p><p><strong>test prompt:</strong> 1girl, &lt;lora:strollWithPet_20230315:0.6&gt;, stroll_with_pet</p><p><strong>test prompt:</strong> 1girl, &lt;lora:strollWithPet_20230315:0.6&gt;, stroll_with_pet, viewer holding leash, leash, all fours</p><p></p><p></p><p>It is recommended to generate a square (512x512) or a long horizontal rectangle (768x512) instead of a long vertical rectangle (512x768).</p><p>This LoRA alone works reasonably well, but if it does not work, add "viewer holding leash, leash, all fours".</p><p>(I think stable diffusion is not good with long strings like oxygen tubes or leash.)</p><p></p><p>The sample images are generated by kohya-trainer/gen_img_diffusers.py by adding 0.6 to this LoRA in Anything3.1.</p><p>Since the train is NAI-based, it would be compatible with other models.</p><p></p>
