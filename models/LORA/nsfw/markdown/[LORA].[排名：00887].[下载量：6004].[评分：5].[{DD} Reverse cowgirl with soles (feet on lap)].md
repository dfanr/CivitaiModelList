### 三、详情
<p>This Lora came out unexpected, I tried to fix <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://civitai.com/models/25759/dd-reverse-cowgirl-anal-frames">this Lora</a>, "cure" it from bad quality artifacts, but understood, that soles are too complicated for SD, and create morbid mutants if left in the dataset. So I thought to cleanse my set from images that containt soles.</p><p>Then I thought, that this pose is beautifull, and it woul be a shame to lose it completely. And then I gathered some additional images with soles and feet, and here we are.</p><p>Well feet are really complicated (try to draw one yourself, to understand that). So expect only 3 out of 10 decent images, or even less - others will be weird.</p><h3>Tags (shouldn't be necessary)</h3><p>Soles, from behind</p><h3>Parameters suggestions</h3><p>Should work in txtToImg with sides somewhere between 512-768</p><p>Weights between 0.5-1</p><p>Clip skip - I didn't use it</p><p>Pay attention to the sampler model - my standard Karras 2M wasn't good all the time. For realistic I found out that dpm adaptive is better (at leasts for me and for that exact Lora). So use x/y/z to find the best combination of parametrs.</p><p></p><p>Enjoy)</p><p>Feel free to ask any questions here in comments, or in <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://discord.gg/9My32sKEAK">my discord channel</a>.</p><p>Also I am making games with AI arts.
