### 三、详情
<p>Creates femboys wearing orange, white, and close enough text on their shirt. Made with ~50 images. Works best at high weights and while it can do somewhat photorealistic, the dataset was primarily anime images. Better results come when using <code>short shorts</code>, <code>croptop</code>, and <code>feminine</code> or <code>otoko no ko</code> (I prefer feminine, but results may vary depending on your model). You can also use <code>restaurant</code> which seems to give better results. <code>bulge</code> will create large lumps in their pants, you may want to use it at a lower weight. All preview images were generated with grapefruitV4</p>
