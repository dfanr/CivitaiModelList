### 三、详情
<p>Lora for Shiranui Mai. This was trained with 饭特稀_v0.8 which you can find on civit. The lora should work ok on similar models but it looks best on 饭特稀_v0.8. Trigger word is "shiranui mai".</p><p>Trained on images from DOA5, DOA6 and a bunch of fan artist renders based on her appearance in those games. I think because of this variance, the lighting feels slightly different than the other doa loras I've made but she still looks pretty good to me.</p><p>Also highly recommend EasyNegative which my sample images are using.</p><p>If you get bad looking faces at lower resolutions try inpainting only the face at higher resolution or using hires fix.</p>
