### 三、详情
<p>Created from the 38 images submitted to the <a rel="ugc" href="https://discord.gg/stablediffusion">Stable Diffusion discord</a> for the Picture of the Week competition "Pixel Infinity".</p><p></p><p>Captions were created manually, training done for 1600 steps at 768x768 on a base of SD1.5.  Trigger phrase is "SDPA pixel art".</p><p><br />Sample images created using a variety of base models - ICBINP, RCNZ Dumb Monkey, Edge of Realism - haven't tried other samplers or step counts as I was really happy with the results straight away</p>
