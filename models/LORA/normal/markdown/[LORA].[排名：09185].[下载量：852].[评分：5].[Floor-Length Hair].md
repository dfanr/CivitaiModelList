### 三、详情
<p>For if the base model hair length isn't long enough. Trained on the animefull-final model for 9 epochs at a network dim of 16.</p><p>Doesn't need any specific tags to work, though putting in very/absurdly long hair in the prompt helps a lot.</p><p>Other hairstyles work to varying degrees of success, haven't had much luck with very long braids but ponytails and twintails generally work pretty well.</p><p>Quality of results may vary, sometimes you'll get a pretty big incomprehensible mess of hair but it's capable of giving some pretty good stuff as well.</p>
