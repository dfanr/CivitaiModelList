### 三、详情
<p>This LORA model is trained on an image of a sanpaku eyes (small iris).</p><p></p><h3>V1.0</h3><p>The iris becomes smaller when the weight is increased. However, if the weight is raised too high, the image becomes rough. 1.2 or lower is better.</p><p>The image of a boy has a smaller iris than that of a girl.</p><p>Angry, surprised, wide-eyed prompts work better.</p><p>For realistic checkpoints, an older, more realistic person is generated.</p><p></p><h3>V2.0</h3><p>In version 2, the training step was increased to generate more smaller iris while using the same input images.</p><p>LoRA weights can now be used with a weight of 1.</p><p>When adapted to realistic models, it produces younger person than version 1(why?).</p><p></p>
