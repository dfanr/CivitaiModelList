### 三、详情
<p>Lora for Tsukushi from doaxvv. This was trained with 饭特稀_v0.8 which you can find on civit. The lora should work ok on similar models but it looks best on 饭特稀_v0.8. Trigger word is "tsukushi".</p><p>Trained with a mix of her with her glasses on and off. The training set had the glasses tagged so unless you specify glasses, images mostly won't have glasses.</p><p>Also highly recommend EasyNegative which my sample images are using.</p><p>If you get bad looking faces at lower resolutions try inpainting only the face at higher resolution or using hires fix.</p>
