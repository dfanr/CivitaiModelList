### 三、详情
<p>I based this model off of the classic art style from movies of the Disney Renaissance (<em>The Little Mermaid</em>, <em>Aladdin</em>, <em>Beauty and the Beast</em>, <em>The Lion King</em>, etc) from 1989 all the way up to 1999. The art style composes of thin outlines, detailed characters and objects, shades and dimensions of colors, and painted backgrounds.</p><p></p><p><strong>(v2.0 UPDATE)</strong>: Fixed the model to become more accurate with locations and people. It's better for you to start off a prompt with this: <strong>disney style (insert subject here), your details</strong></p><p></p><p>Recommended settings:</p><p><strong>Sampler</strong>: DPM++ 2M Karras, DPM++ SDE Karras, or Euler a (three of the best samplers, imo)</p><p><strong>LoRA weight</strong>: ~0.8-1</p><p><strong>CFG scale</strong>: ~5.5-7</p><p>If you are doing hires. fix, I recommend the <strong>denoising weight of ~0.55-0.7</strong>, since if you go further, the model tends to become more realistic and not as cartoony.</p><p>When it comes to locations, use the bracket signs ( ) to add weight to your prompt, just so you can have a better chance for getting the locations to you want.</p><p></p><p>Make sure to add the trigger word "disney style" to activate it.</p><p></p><p>It's optional, but just in case, if your images result in a realistic photo, just add the words:</p><p><strong>cartoon, disney style </strong></p><p></p><p>Checkpoints: I recommend Deliberate since that was the only model that was able to match the Disney art style more accurately, but you can try different styles to your liking.</p><p></p><p><strong>Negative prompt that works well:</strong></p><p>deformed, bad anatomy, disfigured, poorly drawn face, mutation, mutated, extra limb, ugly, disgusting, poorly drawn hands, missing limb, floating limbs, disconnected limbs, malformed hands, blurry, ((mutated hands and fingers)), duplicate images, duplicate, (deformed, distorted, disfigured:1.3), poorly drawn, bad anatomy, wrong anatomy, extra limb, missing limb, floating limbs, (mutated hands and fingers:1.4), disconnected limbs, mutation, mutated, ugly, disgusting, blurry, amputation, EasyNegative, ng_deepnegative_v1_75t</p><p></p><p>DeepNegative and EasyNegative are very recommended.</p><p></p><p>This is only the first version, and I will iron out any problems in the next ones.</p><p></p><p>Suggestions and feedback are welcome! I hope you enjoy!</p>
