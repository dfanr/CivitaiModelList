### 三、详情
<p>This is a simple LoRA aimed at generating dungeon backgrounds. I know some base models are able to do it, but they do it inconsistently and not with the more underground / dark look I was going for. See example images for comparison with base model.</p><p>Use weight 1.0 or more. Trigger word: <strong><em>dungeon</em></strong>. I recommend prompting other tags to help describe what you want to generate. You can be creative, but here's a few suggestions: <em>candle, torch, fire, dark, darkness, backlighting, shadow, stairs, hallway, room, cell</em>, etc. Use different aspect ratios and describe the composition and character placement.</p><p>You can use other loras, for characters, style, concepts, etc. The dataset included scenery images but also some anime NSFW images. See my posts in the gallery.</p><p><u>Additional info</u></p><p>I make these models for fun and for the challenge and release them for free for everyone to enjoy, for non-commercial use (see license). The one thing I ask of you is to provide rating and feedback! That helps me understand where I can improve and helps me stay motivated to produce more content!</p><p>I accept suggestions or requests here or on Civitai discord server.</p><p>You can also check my more polished images and upcoming models on my <a target="_blank" rel="ugc" href="https://www.pixiv.net/en/users/25545070">pixiv </a>profile.</p>
