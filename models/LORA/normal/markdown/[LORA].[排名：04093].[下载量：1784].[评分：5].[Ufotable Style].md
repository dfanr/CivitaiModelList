### 三、详情
<p>This LoRA allows you to generate images in the style of an Ufotable Anime, like the Kara no Kyoukai or the Fate/Stay Night Heaven's Feel movies.</p><p></p><p>This LoRA has been tested on various models, the best results come from NAI, Anythingv4.5 and Anythingv5.</p><p>Best results are obtained by using this LoRA with the NAI model, and Sampling Method Euler A, it generates images really close to the style of Ufotable.<br />Anythingv4.5 seems to be less influenced by the LoRA, and it just gets the shading right most of the time without the art style of Ufotable.</p><p></p><p>The trigger word is <code>ufotable screen cap</code>, and, as of v2 of the LoRA, I recommend using this LoRA with a weight of 1.0.<br /></p><p>This LoRA was trained on approximatively 300 screenshots from the three Fate/Stay Night Heaven's Feel movies.</p>
