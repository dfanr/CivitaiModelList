### 三、详情
<p>Lora for Kasumi from doa series. This was trained with 饭特稀_v0.8 which you can find on civit. The lora should work ok on similar models but it looks best on 饭特稀_v0.8. Trigger word is "kasumi".</p><p>v1 - Mainly trained on her long hair hairstyle.</p><p>v1.1 - Thought kasumi's face could look more accurate, so retrained her with higher resolution images of her face. This one had more of a variety of hairstyles though. Look at comparison image to see if you want the new one.</p><p>Also highly recommend EasyNegative which my sample images are using.</p><p>If you get bad looking faces at lower resolutions try inpainting only the face at higher resolution or using hires fix.</p>
