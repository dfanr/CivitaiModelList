### 三、详情
<p><strong>BlindMask v1:</strong> </p><p>Use at full strength to add an intricate blind mask to your character. Lowering the strength seems to expose more of the face and sometimes adds matching accessories such as headbands or hair clips. Test also show that styling details of the mask are sometimes carried into other elements, commonly other articles of clothing. </p><p>I tested this with a handful of my favorite models and all produces usable results. Some models force visible eyes back into the mask. If you are using the word "blind" in your prompt it may generate deformities with eye... interesting for dark or horror themes. Less pleasing for others!</p><p></p><p>Note on source:</p><p>Most of the training images where pulled from <u>tuahadedana</u> on etsy and instagram.
