### 三、详情
<p>Lora for Ayane from doa series. This was trained with 饭特稀_v0.8 which you can find on civit. The lora should work ok on similar models but it looks best on 饭特稀_v0.8. Trigger word is "ayane".</p><p>Not trained on any specific hairstyle, except I didn't use any images with her in a hairband.</p><p>Also highly recommend EasyNegative which my sample images are using.</p><p>If you get bad looking faces at lower resolutions try inpainting only the face at higher resolution or using hires fix. This is especially important for Ayane since at lower resolutions the red in her pupils will bleed into her eyes and she'll look super baked.</p>
