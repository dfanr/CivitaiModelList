### 三、详情
<p>Lora for Honoka from doa series. This was trained with 饭特稀_v0.8 which you can find on civit. The lora should work ok on similar models but it looks best on 饭特稀_v0.8. Trigger word is "honoka".</p><p>Not trained on any particular costume. Mostly trained on images from doaxvv.</p><p>Training set had mostly her classic side tail and with some of her longer hair down hairstyle. The lora also seems to give her twintails every once in a while.</p><p>Also highly recommend EasyNegative which my sample images are using. If you get bad looking faces at lower resolutions try inpainting only the face at higher resolution or using hires fix.</p>
