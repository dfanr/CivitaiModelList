<p>Lora for amy from doaxvv. This was trained with 饭特稀_v0.8 which you can find on civit. The lora should work ok on similar models but it looks best on 饭特稀_v0.8. Trigger word is "amy".</p><p>Not trained on any particular costume.</p><p><strong>NOTE: </strong>The training set had handful where one of her pigtails was covered by her head, so the model seems to get confused with her hairstyle, and likes to give her a ponytail. I see it just giving her long hair a lot as well. I might go back and retrain the model on better images at some point. But for now I think it gets her face and swept bangs pretty well.</p><p>Also highly recommend EasyNegative which my sample images are using.</p>
