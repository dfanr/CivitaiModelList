### 三、详情
<p>Lora for Nanami from doa. This was trained with 饭特稀_v0.8 which you can find on civit. The lora should work ok on similar models but it looks best on 饭特稀_v0.8. Trigger word is "nanami".</p><p>Her highlights seem pretty subtle from the lora but overall I think it got her face pretty well. Some of the training images had red highlights so you may get see that rarely too.</p><p>Also highly recommend EasyNegative which my sample images are using.</p><p>If you get bad looking faces at lower resolutions try inpainting only the face at higher resolution or using hires fix.</p>
