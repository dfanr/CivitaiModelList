### 三、详情
<p>Lora for NiCO from doa6. This was trained with 饭特稀_v0.8 which you can find on civit. The lora should work ok on similar models but it looks best on 饭特稀_v0.8. Trigger word is "nico".</p><p>She has a tendency to hold up her hands near her face because a lot of sample images had that. Was harder to find good variety of training images online for her compared to other doa girls.</p><p>Her glasses can be prompted with "glasses".</p><p>Also highly recommend EasyNegative which my sample images are using.</p><p>If you get bad looking faces at lower resolutions try inpainting only the face at higher resolution or using hires fix.</p>
