### 三、详情
<p>This is my first Lora that I have trained successfully. </p><p>It is trained on official art from Little Busters 10th Anniversary artbook and White Fairy artbook, as well as CG's and sprites from Little Busters and Kud Wafter games. </p><p>It does some weird things sometimes but it is usable and can produce cute pictures. </p><p>I will improve it as I learn more.</p><p>The trigger word is 'kud', her hat and mantle can be called with 'kud_hat' and 'kud_cape'.</p><p>'school_uniform' will call her uniform, but it may be winter or summer variant.</p><p>'Hair ornament' will call her hair pin, but it does not work very well.</p><p>If anyone knows where I can get scans of the 'Kosmonaut' artbook from the OVA limited edition I would like to add it to the dataset, but it is very hard to find online. Not even a picture of the cover. </p>
