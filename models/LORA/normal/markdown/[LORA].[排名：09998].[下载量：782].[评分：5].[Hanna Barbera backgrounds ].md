### 三、详情
<p>With this model, you can make the iconic backgrounds of early Hanna Barbera cartoons. It's not trained on character design, in fact no image has any character in it, you'll need seperate models for that.</p><p></p><p>Don't expect it to be decent, I literally swiped through a 15 year old blog for the nature model, and sat through the first season of the Huckleberry Hound show and screenshotted anything I found "acceptable" for the civilization one.</p><p></p><p>The models are split into two, civilization and nature, because training them together produced mediocre results. The best results can be done with both of these in conjunction with varying weights.</p><p></p><p>Do not download if you don't want to edit your images afterwards :D</p><p></p><p>By the way, I see at least some liked the model, if you generate some awesome things, you can share, I'd love to see :D</p>
