### 三、详情
<p>Everybody loves striped thigh high socks, right?  Unfortunately, I found a great number of the excellent checkpoints on this site have a devil of a time rendering actual stripes on stockings.  So, I made this LoRA to try and fix that.  This is just the version 1 of this.  I am sure I will come back later, do another version and maybe even combine versions 1 and 2 for a super-LoRA!  </p><p></p><p>Trigger word is stripedsocks.  I recommend using "[color1] striped socks" where color1 is whatever color you want.  Usually, with that prompt, the models will do Color + White stripes.  If you do "[Color 1] and [Color 2] striped socks", that works about 80% of the time.  If you go with three or more colors, though, the patterning goes all weird and you get what you get.</p><p>I have tested with several different models and it worked well with each.  Recommend a strength of 0.75-1.  </p>
