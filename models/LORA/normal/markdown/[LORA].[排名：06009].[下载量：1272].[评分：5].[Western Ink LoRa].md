### 三、详情
<p>Trained within Realistic Vision 1.4 on a selection of western comic book inking and linework.</p><p>Regular prompts work fine even with negative drawing tags, it just makes the output a more realistic comic style.</p><p><strong><u>Doesn't</u></strong> need invocation but if it does slip and you need it to try overpower anything leaking, you can add "wnkrt" to the prompt.</p><p>Seems to do alright at 1 strength for most prompts in SD 1.5 bases, but dialing down to 0.9 will allow more creativity and potentially even cooler stuff.</p><p>If you're still getting colour leaks, try prefix your prompt with "Black and white picture of" or "Monochrome"<br />As a last resort if nothing else works you can begin prefixing with drawing specific modifiers like "drawing" "inking" etc, just enough to kick the initial aesthetic off so the LoRa can take over.</p><p><br />Have not done extensive testing in anime mixes.</p>
