### 三、详情
<p><strong>Firis Mislud character LoRA</strong></p><p>Training based on the official art book and in-game 3D model.</p><p>Having very good facial and hair generation results.</p><p></p><p>Firis clothes are more complex than other Atelier characters. So, when you generate your images, clothes may have missing or wrong detail.</p><p>The cause of this problem is mainly due to the Stable Diffusion model used to generate the images and my training set.</p><p>Firis's original official concept artworks, due to the artist's style, there are errors and missing details in many of them, which can be easily learned by the machine. Moreover, the colors and lines in the original paintings are often not particularly obvious.</p><p>P.S. I plan to redraw some training set images manually to improve training results.</p>
