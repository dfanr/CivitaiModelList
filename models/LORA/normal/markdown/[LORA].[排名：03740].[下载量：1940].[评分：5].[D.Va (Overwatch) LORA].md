<p>My fifth Overwatch LORA. Mainly focused on original appearance + a bit of Academia skin.</p><ul><li><p><strong>Original </strong>- prompt is <em>d.va , </em>I chose not to burn in her hair fully so you more easily could change hairstyles (for example <em>ponytail)</em>, trigger for authentic one is simply <em>long hair.</em></p></li><li><p><strong>Academia </strong>- <em>d.va, alternate hairstyle, twin braids, blazer, bespectacled, glasses, school uniform, pleated skirt</em></p></li></ul><p>Checked strength range is 0.6-0.8.</p><p>Slight issue is headphones - there's barely any images, let alone good ones with D.Va without her headphones.
